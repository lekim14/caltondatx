Indoor Mall Audience Measurement

Discover better understanding of customer patterns, preferences, and interactions within the mall. By analyzing foot traffic, dwell times, and engagement with different areas, mall owners, tenants, and marketers can better understand customer patterns. The result is a more efficient, personalized environment that boosts satisfaction, increases foot traffic, and drives sales, fostering stronger brand loyalty and growth for both retailers and the mall.

Vehicle Audience Measurement

Get valuable insights into the effectiveness of vehicle- based advertising by tracking how these ads are seen, engaged with, and how they influence customer behavior while people are in or around moving vehicles. This data helps advertisers refine their messaging, optimize ad placement, and measure the true impact of vehicle- based campaigns, ultimately driving more meaningful customer engagement and improving the ROI of mobile advertising efforts.
